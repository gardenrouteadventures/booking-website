Our Story

Built on Passion for Motorcycles and the Garden Route

Garden Route Adventure was born from a simple idea: combine the thrill of riding premium motorcycles with the breathtaking beauty of South Africa's most scenic coastal region.

What started as a passion project has grown into a premier motorcycle tourism brand, offering world-class Harley-Davidson tours and adventure bike rentals along the Garden Route.

Our team of local experts has spent years exploring every curve, pass, and viewpoint, curating routes that showcase the very best of what this region has to offer.
