Terms & Conditions

Last updated: July 15, 2026

1. Rental Agreement Terms

By renting a motorcycle from Garden Route Adventure, you agree to the following terms:

  • You must hold a valid motorcycle license valid for the country of operation.
  • You must be at least 25 years of age (or 21 with surcharge).
  • A refundable security deposit is required at the time of rental.
  • Basic third-party liability insurance is included. Damage waiver is optional.
  • The motorcycle must be returned in the same condition it was received.

2. Tour Booking Terms

When booking a tour with Garden Route Adventure, you agree to the following:

  • A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking.
  • Full payment is required before tour departure.
  • All riders must hold a valid motorcycle license.
  • Helmets are mandatory and provided if required.
  • Routes may be adjusted due to weather or safety considerations.

3. Cancellation and Refund Policy

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • 14+ days before: Full refund
  • 7-13 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 7 days: No refund
  • In case of bad weather, tours will be cancelled with a full refund or rescheduled.

4. Waiver and Release of Liability

All participants must sign a digital waiver before participating in any tour or rental. By signing, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • Motorcycle riding involves inherent risks including serious injury or death.
  • You assume all risks associated with motorcycle riding.
  • You release Garden Route Adventure from any liability for injury, loss, or damage.
  • You agree to follow all safety instructions provided.

5. Insurance

Basic third-party liability insurance is included in all tours and rentals. Damage waiver is available at an additional cost. Personal travel insurance is recommended.

6. Privacy Policy

We collect personal information to process bookings and provide services. Your information is protected and never shared with third parties without your consent. See our full privacy policy.

  • Prices are subject to change without notice.
  • All vehicles are subject to availability.
