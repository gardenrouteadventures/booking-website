FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our tours, rentals, bookings, and policies.

What's included in a tour?

Our tours include:

  • Premium Harley-Davidson motorcycle
  • Professional tour guide
  • Fuel for the duration of the tour
  • Helmet (if required)
  • Refreshments and snacks
  • All entrance fees to attractions
  • Basic insurance
  • Professional photography (on select tours)
What's NOT included in a tour?

The following are not included in our tour packages:

  • Personal travel insurance
  • Gratuities for guides
  • Personal items and souvenirs
  • Meals not specified in the tour description
  • Alcoholic beverages (unless specified)
Can I be a passenger on a tour?

Yes! We offer chauffeured rides where you can ride as a pillion on a Harley with an expert rider. This is perfect for those who want to experience the thrill of a motorcycle tour without riding themselves. The chauffeured ride add-on costs R1,500.

What types of bikes are available for rental?

Our rental fleet includes premium adventure bikes:

  • Standard Adventure Bikes: BMW GS 1200, KTM 790 Adventure, Honda Africa Twin, Suzuki V-Strom (R1,500 – R2,500/day)
  • Premium Adventure Bikes: BMW GS 1250, KTM 1290 Super Adventure (R2,500 – R3,500/day)

Harley-Davidsons are available exclusively through our guided tours.

What are the rental requirements?

To rent a motorcycle from us, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Hold a valid motorcycle license
  • Be at least 25 years of age (21 with surcharge)
  • Provide a valid ID or passport
  • Provide a refundable security deposit
What's included in a rental?

Our rentals include:

  • Premium adventure motorcycle
  • Helmet (if required)
  • Basic insurance (damage waiver available at extra cost)
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • Route maps and recommendations
  • Unlimited mileage
How do I book a tour or rental?

Booking is easy! You can:

  1. Visit our Booking Page
  2. Select your preferred experience (tour or rental)
  3. Choose your date and time
  4. Fill in your rider details
  5. Select any add-ons you'd like
  6. Complete your payment
  7. Receive instant confirmation via email and SMS

You'll also receive a digital waiver to sign and an invoice for your records.

What's the deposit requirement?

A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance can be paid on the day of your tour or rental.

What's the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • 14+ days before: Full refund
  • 7-13 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 7 days: No refund

In case of bad weather, tours will be cancelled with a full refund or rescheduled at your convenience.

Is insurance included?

Basic third-party liability insurance is included in all tours and rentals. For rentals, a damage waiver is available at an additional cost (R200 – R500/day).

We recommend taking out personal travel insurance to cover any unforeseen circumstances.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Safety is our top priority. We have the following measures in place:

  • Pre-ride safety briefings before every tour and rental
  • Helmets provided and mandatory
  • Support vehicle follows every tour with a spare bike
  • First aid kit in every support vehicle
  • Staff are first aid trained
  • Emergency contact forms for every rider
  • Breakdown procedure in place
  • SOS emergency on board
What happens in bad weather?

If weather conditions are unsafe for riding, we will cancel the tour with a full refund. You can also choose to reschedule your tour for another date at no additional cost.

Do you provide riding jackets and gear?

We provide thicker riding jackets for guests who need them, especially during the winter months. We recommend bringing your own riding gear for the best fit and comfort, but we have essentials available if needed.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help. Get in touch with us and we'll be happy to assist.
